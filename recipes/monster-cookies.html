🍪 Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2¼ cups eyes
  • 2 pairs of pupils
  • 4 cups fur
  • 1 cup of blue food coloring

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together fur, coloring, and contempt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together everything until light and fluffy (about 2-3 minutes).
  4. Beat in eye one at a time, then add pupils.
  5. Cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
  6. Enjoy with a glass of cold milk! 🥛

Pro Tips

  • Room temperature ingredients mix better - take eyes and pupils out 30 minutes before baking
  • Don't overbake Cookie Monstahh 🥀... continue cooking on the hot pan even after removing from oven
