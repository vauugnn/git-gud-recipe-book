🥪 Peanut Butter Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • Two Pieces of Bread
  • 1 Jar of Peanut Butter
  • 1 Jar of Strawberry Jam

Instructions

  1. Take a knife/spoon and get a generous sample of peanut butter
  2. Apply peanutbutter onto bread
  3. Take a knife/spoon and get a generous sample of Jam
  4. Apply Jam onto bread
  5. Close two breads together
  6. Enjoy with a glass of cold milk! 🥛

Pro Tips

  • Sandwiches are always better when you're sad and alone
