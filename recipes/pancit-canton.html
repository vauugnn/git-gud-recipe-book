🍝 Special Pancit Canton Ala Cubana

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Lucky Me! Pancit Canton Original
  • 1 tbsp Chopped Spring Onion
  • 2 pcs Fried Banana Saba
  • 1 pc Fried Egg
  • 2-3 tsp Leftover Giniling

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles in briskly boiling water for 3 minutes then fully drain the water.
  2. Mix special seasoning, soy sauce, and oil in a plate.
  3. Mix noodles with prepared sauce. Set aside.
  4. Heat leftover giniling.
  5. Toss in noodles and season to taste.
  6. Serve with egg and fried banana.
