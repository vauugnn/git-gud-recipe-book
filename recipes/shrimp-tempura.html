🍤Golden Shrimp Tempura

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo large shrimps
  • 1 Tbsp. calamansi juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • cooking oil for deep frying
  • 1 pc egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. MSG
  • 1 cup fish or shrimp broth
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice wine
  • 2 tsp. sugar
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated radish or labanos

Instructions

  1. Remove the head and shell of shrimps but leave tail intact. Devein.
  2. Make crosswise slit on inner side of shrimp, so it would not curl when cooked.
  3. Season with salt and calamansi juice. Set aside.
  4. Beat the egg. Add water and beat again. Add all the flour, baking powder, salt and MSG.
  5. In a deep pan, heat about 2 inches deep cooking oil. Dip shrimps in batter and drop one by one in hot oil.
  6. Fry until golden brown. Serve hot with tempura sauce.

Pro Tips

  • Room temperature ingredients mix better - take eggs and butter out 30 minutes before baking
  • Don't overbake! Cookies continue cooking on the hot pan even after removing from oven
  • For chewier cookies, slightly underbake them. For crispier cookies, bake a minute longer
